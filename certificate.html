Project Overview

AI Voice-Controlled Robot Car

I built an AI Voice-Controlled Robot Car as my college exhibition project, organized by Bhaktapur Municipality on 25 December 2024.

The robot is capable of being controlled using voice commands such as “forward”, “left”, “right”, and “stop”. It demonstrates the integration of AI and robotics, making it an interactive and modern system suitable for real-world automation applications.
